FAQ

What kind of wedding gift are you expecting?

The best gift you can give us is your presence and your time; it sounds cheesy but it's true. If you still want to give us something final help to cover the wedding's cost is always appreciated

Is there a dress code?

I mean, it's a wedding and it's on October so if you come in shorts you will not fit plus you'll be cold

Speaking of cold, what kind of weather do you have in October in Pamplona?

Do I look like a Meteorologist? I guess chilly, bring a jacket.

Will there be a party and free drinks?

Yes and yes (as long as the COVID rules allow it).

Is it mandatory to dance?

Yes.

Until when do I have time to RSVP?

We would like to have the participants list finalized by the 1st of July 2022.

How many participants will there be?

We are trying to keep it under 50 75 100 people.

Can we take pictures and videos during the wedding?

As long as you don't get in the official photographer's way and you don't bring something crazy like a drone, sure, no problem

Are you going on a honey moon after the wedding?

We are not sure yet but we are planning to go to New Zeland for a month

Which one of you made this website?

We did it both 50-50 and Montse is not currently looking over my shoulder.
